Interest in IT

What is your interest in IT? When did your interest in IT start? Was there a particular event or person that sparked your interest? Outline your IT experience (if any).

My interest in IT is to either do a Data Scientist or Cyber Security. My interest in IT started when I saw the people around me just talking about technology and how it is impacting our lives daily. The debate usually goes whether it’s ethical or moral the way we are moving forward. We have people scared and frightened about how advance our technology is and how we can basically find out what is going on the other side of the world with our fingertips. The question stood in front of me, what is this thing. Why are the companies choosing to opt technology and some individual having a hard time to understand the evolution of IT. These sorts of questions always came up on regular basis during social events in my family. Therefore, I decided to learn it, to truly understand What is IT.
